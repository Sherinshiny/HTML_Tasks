What is a paragraph?

A Paragraph is a group of related sentencews about a single topic. The topic is one, and only one idea.

M.A.Adam

A paragraph has three main parts:

  1. Topic sentence: It is the first sentence in a paragraph. It names the topic and tells what the paragraph will explain.
  2. Supporting sentences: They are the middle sentence in a paragraph. they give examples or other details about the topic.
  3. Concluding sentence: It is the last sentence in a paragraph. It often repeat the topic sentence in different words or summarize the main points.
